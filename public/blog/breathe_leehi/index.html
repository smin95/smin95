Breathe by Lee hi

PUBLISHED ON MAY 18, 2020

Breathe is one of my favorite songs. Many covers have been recorded. Here are my favorites.

Original by Lee hi

This is the original recording of the song.

Han Dong Geun

Han’s control of dynamic is amazing. His arrangement strays from the original but is absolutely beautiful.

Kyu Hyun

His voice is beautiful. Very clear and resonant. This rendition is very faithful to the original
